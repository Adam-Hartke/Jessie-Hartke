Somewhere Fest & Conference

Role: Program Lead and Operations Owner Timeframe: 2023–Present Scope: Multi-day, multi-venue festival and conference program

Overview

Led end-to-end program delivery for a large-scale festival and conference operating under fixed deadlines, real budget constraints, and a highly complex stakeholder environment.

Scope included live music programming, conference content, and multi-venue operations, requiring coordination across artists, venues, vendors, sponsors, nonprofit partners, and external production teams.

Program evolved year over year while maintaining full operational delivery and execution standards.

Role & Ownership

Owned the integrated program across operations, finance, programming, and marketing, acting as the central point of coordination and decision-making.

  • Built and managed the master program plan across all workstreams
  • Owned timelines, dependencies, and execution cadence across teams
  • Managed multi-million-dollar budgets, forecasts, and vendor commitments
  • Coordinated internal teams and external partners across multiple venues
  • Served as primary escalation point for risk, scope changes, and execution decisions

Execution

  • Built and maintained a single source of truth across creative, operational, and financial timelines
  • Designed workflows aligning artists, venues, production, marketing, and sponsorship deliverables
  • Implemented budget tracking and financial controls to manage spend against forecast
  • Led weekly cross-functional operating rhythms to surface risks and unblock teams
  • Coordinated venue logistics, staffing, and production schedules across parallel locations
  • Managed late-stage changes without compromising critical path timelines

Outcomes

  • Delivered a multi-day, multi-venue festival and conference on a fixed, immovable deadline
  • Supported 150+ artists and speakers across multiple stages and venues
  • Maintained financial discipline within a constrained and evolving budget environment
  • Established repeatable operating structures and workflows for future iterations
  • Improved cross-functional alignment across creative, operational, and financial teams

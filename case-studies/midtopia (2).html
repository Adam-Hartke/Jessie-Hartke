Midtopia

Role: Co-Founder and Operations Lead Timeframe: 2022–Present Scope: Zero-to-one organization build, operating model design, and cross-functional execution

Overview

Co-founded and led operations for a mission-driven organization supporting independent artists through education, programming, and strategic partnerships.

Built and operated the organization from the ground up in a fragmented, post-pandemic environment shaped by funding volatility, market consolidation, and shifting stakeholder expectations.

Role & Ownership

Owned operating model design and execution across programs, partnerships, and financial oversight.

  • Designed and led cross-functional initiatives spanning education, events, partnerships, and artist support
  • Established governance structures, decision-making frameworks, and operating rhythms
  • Owned budgets, forecasts, and financial planning across programs
  • Coordinated funders, partners, internal contributors, and community stakeholders
  • Translated evolving strategy into executable plans under changing constraints

Execution

  • Designed operating models and program structures to support repeatable execution
  • Built planning systems, timelines, and dependency mapping across parallel initiatives
  • Implemented budgeting, forecasting, and reporting to maintain financial discipline
  • Led cross-functional coordination across programming, marketing, operations, and partnerships
  • Managed execution through shifting funding conditions and external dependencies
  • Iterated programs based on capacity, outcomes, and operational reality

Outcomes

  • Launched and operated multiple cross-functional programs supporting artists and community partners
  • Built durable operating systems enabling consistent execution across initiatives
  • Improved alignment and decision-making through defined governance structures
  • Maintained financial oversight within a constrained and evolving funding environment
  • Created transition frameworks supporting continuity and long-term sustainability decisions
